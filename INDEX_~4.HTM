Frequently asked questions.

If you don't see what you're looking for, the calculator answers it faster than this page can.

Does Profitsy work for both Etsy and Shopify sellers?

Yes. Profitsy is built for print-on-demand sellers running on Etsy, Shopify, or both. Most POD sellers sell on multiple platforms — Profitsy handles the fee math for each so you see real profit on either side.

Why is the bulk order calculator important?

Bulk orders are where POD sellers make extra profit. Wedding party shirts, corporate gift orders, and multi-pack listings can earn far more per order than single sales — but only if you price them right. Profitsy calculates exactly what to charge so you never undercharge.

What's the daily sale preview?

An easy-to-read graph that shows your real profit at different sale discount levels — 15% off, 20% off, 25% off — so you can pick a daily sale percentage that still pays. See exactly when a discount starts eating your margin instead of growing your shop.

Yes. Profitsy is fully responsive and runs in any mobile browser, so you can price bulk orders from your phone anywhere. Native iOS and Android apps are coming soon.

Is Profitsy free to use?

Do I need to connect my Etsy or Shopify store?

No. Profitsy works without any store connection. Type in product details and get the math in seconds — no API setup, no permissions.
